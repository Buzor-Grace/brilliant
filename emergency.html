Nigerian Police Emergency Phone Numbers

In Nigeria, the general emergency phone number is 112 or 199 and these numbers can be called in most part of Nigeria when there is any emergency situation from the police, fire department or ambulance service. However if you need to contact the Nigerian Police Force directly, the emergency numbers to contact them can be found for various states in Nigeria below.
